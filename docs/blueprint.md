# **App Name**: Dove Jobs

## Core Features:

- Job Listing: Allow employers to post job openings with detailed descriptions and requirements.
- Job Search: Enable job seekers to search for jobs based on keywords, location, category and other filters.
- User Accounts: Allow both job seekers and employers to create and manage their profiles.
- Job Application: Enable job seekers to apply for jobs directly through the platform.
- AI-Powered Job Matching: An AI tool that recommends relevant job listings to job seekers based on their skills, experience, and preferences.
- Employer Dashboard: Provide employers with a dashboard to manage their job postings and review applications.
- Messaging System: A secure messaging system so candidates and companies can ask and respond to questions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Sky blue (#7DD3FC) to reflect the aspirational theme and limitless opportunities of the platform.
- Background color: Light blue-gray (#F0F4F8), offering a neutral, calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Light Purple (#A855F7), used for CTAs to draw attention
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a versatile sans-serif font for a clean and modern look.
- Use clean, modern icons to represent different job categories and functionalities.
- Maintain a clean and intuitive layout with clear sections for job listings, search functionality, and user profiles.
- Incorporate subtle animations, such as transitions and loading indicators, to enhance user experience.
